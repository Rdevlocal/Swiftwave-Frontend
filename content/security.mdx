# Security Policy

## Introduction
At Swiftwave, we prioritize the security of our users' data and the integrity of our systems. This document outlines our commitment to maintaining a secure environment and the practices we implement to achieve this goal.

## Data Protection
We implement various measures to ensure the protection of your data, including:

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols.
- **Access Controls**: Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only, based on their role within the organization.
- **Regular Audits**: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks.

## Compliance
Swiftwave is committed to complying with all applicable data protection laws and regulations, including:

- **GDPR**: We adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to ensure the rights of our users in the European Union.
- **CCPA**: We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), giving California residents rights regarding their personal information.

## Incident Response
In the event of a security breach, we have an incident response plan in place to quickly address and mitigate any potential impact. This includes:

- **Immediate Investigation**: Our security team will investigate the breach to determine the cause and extent of the impact.
- **User Notification**: Affected users will be notified promptly, along with guidance on steps they should take to protect their data.
- **Remediation**: We will implement measures to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future.

## Conclusion
Swiftwave is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of security. We regularly review and update our security policies and practices to adapt to the evolving threat landscape. For any questions or concerns regarding our security policy, please contact us at support@swiftwave.io.
